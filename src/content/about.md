# PDWN

PDWN (Personal Data Watch & Neutralize) is a local desktop application that helps identify personal data and technical secrets in files.

## What the app does

- Scans selected folders locally.
- Detects personal data categories and technical secrets.
- Assigns risk levels and explains why a file is risky.
- Supports custom detection types defined in YAML.

## Privacy by design

- Analysis runs on your machine.
- Revealed values are not persisted in reports.
- You can ignore values you do not want to detect again.
